## What is Necomify?

Necomify is a simple Next.js E-Commerce starter kit that helps you build your online store with ease. It comes with pre-built pages, without styles, ensuring that you can customize to your needs.

## Why Necomify?

1. **Easy to Customize**: Necomify is built without any styles out of the box, making it easy to customize to your needs.
2. **Built with Next.js**: Necomify is built with Next.js, a React framework that makes it easy to build fast and scalable web applications.
3. **Endless Options**: With Necomify, you are not bound to any specific design or functionality. You can build your store the way you want, swap out dependencies such as AppWrite, and more.
